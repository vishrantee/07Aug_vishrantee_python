About Us

A cup full of happiness for your soul

TEA!

Since the time immemorial, the world has been mesmerized by this beautiful beverage called Chai. It started somewhere around the North-Eastern areas of India and now it is the most consumed drink in the world, after water. India is the country of tea lovers. And they all love Chai from the bottom of their hearts. Tea Post was envisioned with the dream of reaching out to such tea lovers and offering them a great experience of having Chai.

According to a study, Indians consume about 25% of the tea produced in the world, every year. And that’s because it makes a big & powerful impact on one’s mind, which is amazingly refreshing. This is evident from the unprecedented rise in the number of Tea Post outlets which grew to 200+ within the first decade. Today, Tea Post is one of the largest Tea Café chains in India — serving more than 15 varieties of Chai and a wide range of delicious snacks.

The beginning of a dream

Though Tea Post was established in the year 2013, the idea of Tea Post had been brewing in the heart of Mr. Darshan Dashani for a long time. It was his comprehension about the demand and vision which took Tea Post from one outlet to 50 outlets within just 3 years. And then going on a hyper drive, Tea Post opened its 100″ outlet in 2017. Starting from a humble beginning, today Tea Post has become one of the fastest-growing Tea Café chains in India — present in 40 cities across 4 states.

A dream called tea post

During my international travels, I noticed that everyone was very comfortable in getting their everyday breakfast and coffee from nearby cafes. This is when I realized that this trend can be brought to India successfully – albeit with an Indian perspective. Then I tweaked the offerings with Indian F & B sensibilities and included Indian Chai and traditional snacks into the menu. And we were all set for a revolution.

- Mr. Darshan Dashani

Celebrating a decade of serving good chai

2013

The founder – Mr. Darshan Dashani launched the first Tea Post in Rajkot city to cater to the Chai lover within everyone.

2014

Tea Post received immense love from the people and within just a year, launched multiple outlets in multiple cities.

2015

The dream of taking Tea Post to the national level begun in 2015 with establishment of a pan – India expansion policy.

2016

Tea Post launched its 50th store amid much fun & fervor within just 3 years, following the vision of Mr. Darshan Dashani.

2017

Tea Post launched its very first garden café – the Desi Café in Rajkot, and at the same time also launched the 100th outlet.

The guarantee of qualitea

At Tea Post, you get the best of everything – be it Chai, Food, or Experience. Especially for the Chai, we take extra care in all the aspects and resources that go into the process. We source the best quality Chai from authentic tea cultivators in Assam and we ensure that they are processed in totally clean & hygienic manner. Also, we create our own tea blends to maintain uniformity in every cup that is served at all the 200+ Tea Posts.

The Chai is also brewed as per a well-defined process carried out by thoroughly trained tea makers. Also, all the ingredient required for flavored Chai and snacks are freshly sourced from the local farmers and traders. At every step, in every process, we ensure that we can boast of the guarantee of the quality.

The cultural connect

Tea Post is the flag bearer in creating a Tea Café experience based on Indian heritage and cultural sensibilities. That’s why our style of making & serving Chai is quite distinct. Also, every Tea Post is designed with quintessentially Indian visual theme and artifacts. We also regularly organize activities and cultural events on Tea Posts across 40 cities to strengthen the cultural connect. Starting from music performance to stand-up comedy, dance performances, aerobics master-class, and so many other activities. We take immense pride in offering tea lovers a chance to experience and appreciate Indian art.

The social commitment

Tea Post has been serving more than 2 million cups of Chai every month and it brings us closer to the local communities every day. In the last decade, we have received immense love from the people and we shall be forever grateful. We established the Tea Post Well Wish Foundation in 2019, to give back love to the society. Through this foundation, we carry out numerous activities and launch initiatives that can improve the standard of living and make a positive impact on local communities.
